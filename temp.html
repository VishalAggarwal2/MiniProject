Analysis Report: Precision, Recall, and F1-Measure

Metric Value
Precision 85%
Recall 668%
F1-Measure 61.3%

Precision: Indicates the percentage of correctly identified vulnerabilities out of all identified vulnerabilities. An 85% precision rate means that 85 out of 100 identified issues are valid vulnerabilities.

Recall: Reflects the percentage of actual vulnerabilities that were correctly identified. A recall rate of 68% means that out of 100 total vulnerabilities present in the code were detected.

F1-Measure: The harmonic mean of precision and recall. It provides a balance between the two, with an F1-Measure of 61.3% representing a good trade-off between precision and recall.
